Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

Program Structure

  • Duration: 3 years
  • Courses: 12 core courses, 6 elective courses
  • Credits: 120 credits

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 18 years or above
  • Education: High school diploma or equivalent
  • Language: English proficiency

Fees and Payment Options

  • Program fee: Rs. 1,300,000
  • Payment options: Installment plans, scholarships available

Learning Methods

  • Online lectures
  • Downloadable resources
  • Exams: Online proctored exams

Career Prospects and Job Opportunities

Graduates can pursue careers in business management, marketing, finance, and more.
